About Rhythm House

Our story, mission, and passion for music

Our Journey

Founded in 1985, Rhythm House began as a small record store in the heart of Mumbai.

What started as a passion project for music enthusiasts quickly grew into one of India's most iconic music destinations. Over the years, we've witnessed the evolution of music formats - from vinyl records to cassettes, CDs, and now digital streaming.

Today, Rhythm House stands as a testament to the enduring love for physical music media, offering an extensive collection of CDs, DVDs, tapes, books, and magazines to music enthusiasts across the country.
